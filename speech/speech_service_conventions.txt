Production service:

    Receives: an id and a delay:
        id -> "scene"_number_speaker_speakerline | "scene"_number_"question"_number_"answer"_number | "repeat"
            number -> "0" | "1" | "2" | ...
            speaker -> "a" | "b" | "s"
            speakerline -> "0" | "1" | "2" | ...
        delay -> in seconds?
    
    Does: play the script line, question or answer with correspondent filename. On the 2 speakers connected to the robot.
            (There will be two services that do this one for each robot)
    
    Returs: Confirms that it has completed the task successfully.
            

Recognition service:

    Receives: an id that describes the situation
        id -> "scene"_number | "done"
            number -> "0" | "1" | "2" | "3" | "4"
        
    Does: Starts listening from the microphone and identifies what has been said based on the situation.
    
    Returns: "scene"_number_"question"_number | "repetition_request" | "done_confirmation" | "time_out"
        number -> "0" | "1" | "2" | ...
